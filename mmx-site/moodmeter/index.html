Coaching Support

GROW Coaching Model

A structured conversation framework to help you move forward, whatever your mood.

  • Goal — What do you want to achieve today, right now?
  • Reality — Where are you honestly at? What's actually happening?
  • Options — What are three things you could do, big or small?
  • Will — What one action will you commit to before end of day?

Situational Coaching

Your current mood signals what type of support might help most right now.

  • High energy (4-5): Stretch yourself — take on a challenge or mentor someone today.
  • Neutral (3): Seek a small win. Completing even one task builds momentum for the rest.
  • Stressed (2): Ask for task prioritization support. You don't have to sort this alone.
  • Burned out (1): Signal to your supervisor. A plan to redistribute load is both valid and professional.

Positive Reinforcement

Recognize what's working — even on hard days, there's something worth acknowledging.

  • Name one thing you did well this week, however small.
  • Notice the effort, not just the outcome. Showing up matters.
  • Thank one person today — gratitude rewires toward the positive.
  • Track your check-in streak. Consistency itself is a win worth celebrating.
